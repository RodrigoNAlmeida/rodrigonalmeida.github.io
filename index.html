Welcome!

My name is Rodrigo Nicolau Almeida and this is my webpage dedicated to research and academics.

I am a PhD student at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation of the University of Amsterdam , working under the supervision of Nick Bezhanishvili.

My masters thesis focused on general Blok-Esakia like phenomena, and specifically about the Goldblatt translation from Orthologic to KTB normal modal logic. My current research is directed to (bi)- intuitonistic logic, with a specific focus on studying various kinds of properties of rule systems (non-standard rules, admissibility, interpolation, amongst others). More generally, I am interested in universal algebra, model theory and point-free topology.

In a previous academic life I was a researcher in sociology of culture. You can find most of my work related to that in this link.
