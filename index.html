Welcome!

My name is Rodrigo Nicolau Almeida and this is my webpage dedicated to research and academics.

I am a PhD student at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation of the University of Amsterdam , working under the supervision of Nick Bezhanishvili.

I have recently completed my masters thesis, which focused on general Blok-Esakia like phenomena, and specifically about the Goldblatt translation from Orthologic to KTB normalmodal logic. My current research is directed to modal intuitonistic logic, in its algebraic and duality-theoretic aspects. More generally, I am interested in universal algebra, model theory and point-free topology.

In a previous academic life I was a researcher in sociology of culture. You can find most of my work related to that in this link.
