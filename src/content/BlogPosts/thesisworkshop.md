The DADL workshop will take place on the 29th of March 2026 in Amsterdam. It aims to connect researchers and students interested in algebra, duality theory and especially definability problems in these topics (uniform interpolation, higher-order quantifier elimination, normal forms, free algebra constructions, etc). Participation is free and no registration is required.

The workshop is co-located with the PhD defense of [Rodrigo Almeida](https://www.uva.nl/en/content/events/2026/03/cycles-with-annotations-non-wellfounded-proof-theory-of-modal-fixpoint-logics.html?origin=Hrba9dP3TiyS8U8plHPUHQ). The defense is public and will be held on the 30 of March at 14 am at the Aula of the University of Amsterdam (Singel 411, Amsterdam).
